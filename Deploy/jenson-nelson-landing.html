About

JENSON NELSON

I'm a designer who started drawing and never really stopped — I just started drawing things that end up on shelves instead of walls. I came up at American Apparel making apparel graphics and production art, moved into high-volume product and packaging design where I was hands-on with everything from color palettes to factory specs, and for the last five years I've been running my own studio making products, brands, and packaging from scratch.

I'm also a painter. That's where the color sense comes from — not a textbook, but thousands of hours mixing paint and seeing what happens. I bring that same eye and that same willingness to get my hands dirty to every design problem I touch.
