What I Believe
What you put on in the morning is a decision about who you are that day. Product is personal — it's armor, expression, identity. I want to be on the team that makes the thing people reach for when they want to feel like themselves. I thrive in creative teams where people push each other, share walls, and make work that's bigger than any one person could build alone. The best design happens in rooms full of makers.
